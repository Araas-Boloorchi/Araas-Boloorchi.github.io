Araas Boloorchi

Ph.D. Computer Science | Machine Learning Engineer
Computer Vision Engineer with Ph.D. in Computer Science and 6+ years developing advanced CV systems and 3D reconstruction pipelines. Deep expertise in 3D computer vision, object detection/segmentation, and real-time scene understanding.

Araas Boloorchi's journey in technology began in high school, where he focused on math and physics. His passion for innovation led him to the University of Science and Culture, where he pursued a degree in software engineering. During his undergraduate years, Araas formed a team that participated in numerous competitions, securing third place in an AI irregular gesture recognition competition.

After earning his bachelor's degree, Araas pursued a master's degree at the University of Qazvin in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. His academic journey then took him to Oklahoma State University for a direct Ph.D. program, where he joined the Advanced Tech Research Center, focusing on Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) and visual-inertial odometry.

Throughout his career, Araas has worked at industry leaders including General Electric, PwC, and Sanborn, gaining valuable experience in machine learning, robotics, computer vision, and software engineering. His research on visual-inertial odometry culminated in published papers that exemplify his commitment to advancing AI technology while ensuring practical applications.
